INGREDIENTS

1 serving quick-cooking or old-fashioned rolled oats
1 pinch of nutmeg
1tablespoon dried cranberries
1tablespoon dried cherries
1tablespoon roasted pistachios
1tablespoon honey
DIRECTIONS

Prepare the oats according to the package directions. Stir in the nutmeg. Top with the cranberries, cherries, and pistachios. Drizzle with the honey.
